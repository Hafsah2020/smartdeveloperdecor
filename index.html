Interior Decoration Furnishing Catalogue

How to Use This Catalogue:

  1. Review: Explore different furniture styles catalogs for each room .
  2. Select: After reviewing the different options, note the style number of the furniture style you like best.
  3. Select: Pick a palette that suits the overall mood and aesthetic you want to achieve in the room.
  4. Provide Feedback: Send the number of the chosen style and the chosen colour palette.
  5. Next Steps: After selecting the style and palette, the furniture pieces will be adjusted to reflect the colours of your chosen palette.
    Each furniture piece (e.g., sofa, armchairs, tables, ottomans) will match the colour theme, maintaining consistency across all items
    We'll fit the final choice to the layout plan and start implemaentation.
